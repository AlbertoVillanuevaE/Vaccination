Preparations Before Vaccine

the success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the
strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work:

Avoid alcoholic beverages

It is prooved that alcoholic breverages could
affect the response of the immune system with
the presence of new antibodies.

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on immune work.
Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol
production and oxidative stress in the body.

Eat healthy food

Eat healthy not only has benefits in how your body
accepts the new antibodies of the COVID-19 vaccine.
But also you will be healthy and will improve your
life span.

Get enough sleep

Sleep well after vaccination strenghtens the
immune system and decrease the probability of
getting any reaction to the vaccine

Exercise or physical activity

At least 90 minutes of excercise after getting
the dose can increase the number of antibodies
