    Why should I vaccine?

    In order to avoid any doubts about getting the COVID-19 vaccine, identify the following 4 benefits of COVID-19 vaccination

    Minimize the spread of viruses

    Vaccine has been proven effect to prevent someone from getting infected with Coronavirus

    Forming antibodies

    COVID-19 vaccine proven to help shape antibody response for immune system

    Protecting people nearby

    The benefits of COVID-19 vaccination are the vaccine that we can also help protect people around us

    Creating group immunity

    Preparations Before Vaccine

    the success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work:

    Avoid alcoholic beverages

    It is prooved that alcoholic breverages could affect the response of the immune system with the presence of new antibodies.

    Avoid stress

    Stress has a profound effect on immune work.
    Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and oxidative stress in the body.

    Eat healthy food

    Eat healthy not only has benefits in how your body accepts the new antibodies of the COVID-19 vaccine. But also you will be healthy and will improve your life span.

    Get enough sleep

    Sleep well after vaccination strenghtens the immune system and decrease the probability of getting any reaction to the vaccine

    Exercise or physical activity

    At least 90 minutes of excercise after getting the dose can increase the number of antibodies
